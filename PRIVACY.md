# WhoopBar Privacy Policy

WhoopBar runs entirely on your own Mac.

- It does **not** collect, transmit, sell, or share your data.
- Your WHOOP data is fetched directly from WHOOP to your Mac and stored only in a local database on your computer (`~/Library/Application Support/WhoopBar`).
- Your WHOOP credentials and access tokens are stored only in a private file on your Mac (`~/Library/Application Support/WhoopBar/credentials.json`, readable only by your user account).
- There are no servers, no analytics, and no third parties.

You can remove everything at any time by deleting the app and the `~/Library/Application Support/WhoopBar` folder.

Questions: open an issue at https://github.com/Mahir-Isikli/whoopbar/issues
